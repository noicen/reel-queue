Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 4, 2026

Reel Queue ("the app") is a personal, single-user application built and used by its creator to queue and upload their own video content to their own TikTok account. It is not a public service, and it is not distributed to or used by other members of the public.

What information is accessed

When connected to TikTok via TikTok Login Kit, the app accesses:

  • Basic public profile information (display name, avatar, open ID) via the user.info.basic scope
  • Permission to upload video content to the connected account via the video.publish scope

How information is used

This information is used solely to authenticate the app's owner with their own TikTok account and to upload videos that the owner has personally selected and queued through the app's dashboard. The app does not access, read, or use any information belonging to any other TikTok user.

How information is stored

Access tokens and basic account identifiers are stored locally on the owner's own device/server, in a local database used only by this app. No information is transmitted to or stored on any third-party server, and no data is sold, shared, or disclosed to any third party.

Video content

Videos uploaded through the app are files the owner created and selected themselves. They are transmitted directly to TikTok's Content Posting API for the sole purpose of delivering them to the owner's own TikTok account.

Data retention and deletion

The owner may delete any stored post, video file, or connected account record at any time directly within the app. Disconnecting the TikTok account removes the locally stored access credentials.

Contact

This app has a single owner/operator. Questions about this policy can be directed to that individual directly.
